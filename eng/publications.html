Financial Support

The German Ministry for Education and Science (Bundesministerium für Bildung und Forschung BMBF) financially supports the Competence Network TDM KJP within its programme for the development of new instruments and methods for patient-oriented medical research.
Title of the project: "Therapeutic Drug Monitoring in child and adolescent psychiatry. Competence network and patient registry for psychopharmacological research: establishment and further development of a multicentre IT-infrastructure."

Society for "Durchführung Neurowissenschaftlicher Tagungen e.V.", Paulsborner-Strasse 44, 14193 Berlin
